ABOUT LUXECO

Luxe.CO is China’s influential online business media for luxury, fashion and lifestyle, with special attention to the intersection of fashion, finance and technology.

Over the past five years, Luxe.CO has become the go-to place for entrepreneurs, investors, professionals, opinion leaders and high-end consumers for the latest and well-curated news and analysis on luxury, fashion and lifestyle brand operation, investment and technological innovation. Also, our app and website constitute a very user-friendly database for in-depth brand knowledge, fashion finance transactions and industry research papers.

Since 2015, Luxe.CO has successfully hosted “Global Fashion Innovation and Investment Forum”, for more details, please visit forum.luxe.co.
